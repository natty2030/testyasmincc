About Us
Yasmina Childcare was established in 2019 by Yasmin Ibrahim. Yasmin graduated in 2006 and received her Associate of Child Development at San Diego City College and has over 15 years of experience proiding childcare services. Yasmin is passionate about providing a safe and nuturing enviorment that children can thrive and grow in. Yasmin worked as an assistant teacher, director, lead teacher, provider, and worked in the State of Minnesota as a Childcare Director in a facility that cared for 70+ children over 6 years.

Mission Statement

It is the mission of Yasmin Childcare to provide a nuturing safe enviorment that provides equitable quality education, a fun enviorment where they can engage with their peers while fostering our children's strengths and challenge their growth.
